
# FinnCenter - Website ban khoa hoc ngon ngu tich hop AI

FinnCenter la website hoc tap va mua ban khoa hoc truc tuyen, tap trung vao cac khoa hoc ngon ngu va on tap ky nang. He thong ho tro nguoi hoc tim kiem khoa hoc, dang ky/mua khoa hoc, hoc theo module/bai hoc, lam bai test cuoi khoa va nhan danh gia bang AI cho cac bai viet.

Website duoc xay dung theo mo hinh LMS ket hop marketplace: giao vien co the tao va quan ly khoa hoc, hoc vien co the mua khoa hoc bang vi noi bo, theo doi tien do hoc tap va lam bai kiem tra sau khi hoan thanh noi dung bai hoc.

## Chuc nang chinh

- Ban va quan ly khoa hoc ngon ngu: hien thi danh sach khoa hoc, chi tiet khoa hoc, combo course, skill training va certification prep.
- Ho tro nhieu ngon ngu: he thong duoc thiet ke cho cac khoa hoc English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean va Vietnamese.
- Dang ky va dang nhap: nguoi dung co the tao tai khoan, dang nhap, dang xuat va khoi phuc mat khau bang OTP gui qua email.
- Phan quyen nguoi dung: gom Student, Teacher va Admin.
- Vi hoc vien: hoc vien co the nap tien, xem so du, lich su giao dich va dung so du de mua khoa hoc.
- Thanh toan VNPAY: he thong tao giao dich nap vi, dieu huong sang cong thanh toan VNPAY va xu ly ket qua tra ve.
- Hoc tap theo tien do: khoa hoc gom module va lesson; he thong ghi nhan bat dau hoc, hoan thanh bai hoc va tinh phan tram tien do.
- Quan ly khoa hoc cho giao vien: giao vien co the tao, sua, khoa/xoa khoa hoc, them module, them lesson va upload video bai hoc.
- Bai test va on tap: giao vien tao test, cau hoi trac nghiem, dung/sai, dien tu, tu luan va cau hoi nghe co audio.
- Lam bai va cham diem: hoc vien chi duoc lam test khi hoan thanh 100% bai hoc; he thong tu cham cau hoi khach quan va luu ket qua lam bai.
- Cham bai viet bang AI: cau hoi tu luan va API essay evaluation duoc cham bang Ollama AI, tra ve diem, nhan xet, loi sai, goi y va ban viet cai thien.
- Chung chi va diem AI: khi hoc vien dat bai test, he thong ghi nhan hoan thanh khoa hoc, gui email chung chi va cong diem AI.

## Cong nghe su dung

- Next.js 16 va React 19 cho giao dien va API routes.
- TypeScript cho ma nguon an toan va de bao tri.
- Prisma 7 voi PostgreSQL cho co so du lieu.
- Nodemailer de gui OTP dat lai mat khau va email chung chi.
- VNPAY de xu ly nap tien vao vi.
- Ollama de cham bai viet bang AI.
- Tailwind CSS cho giao dien.

## Doi tuong su dung

- Hoc vien: dang ky tai khoan, mua khoa hoc, hoc bai, lam test, xem tien do, nhan chung chi va su dung tinh nang AI.
- Giao vien: tao va quan ly khoa hoc, module, lesson, video, bai test va cau hoi.
- Quan tri vien: co quyen truy cap va quan ly rong hon trong he thong.

## Tong quan luong hoat dong

Nguoi hoc tao tai khoan va dang nhap vao website. Sau do, hoc vien co the xem danh sach khoa hoc, loc theo ngon ngu/loai khoa hoc, nap tien vao vi va mua khoa hoc mong muon. Khi da dang ky khoa hoc, hoc vien hoc theo tung bai, he thong ghi nhan tien do va mo khoa bai test khi tien do dat 100%.

Sau khi hoc vien nop bai test, he thong cham diem tu dong. Voi cau hoi tu luan, he thong goi AI de danh gia noi dung bai viet theo cac tieu chi nhu ngu phap, tu vung, mach lac va muc do dap ung de bai. Neu hoc vien dat diem qua mon, he thong danh dau hoan thanh khoa hoc, cong diem AI va gui email chung chi.

## Diem noi bat

FinnCenter khong chi la website ban khoa hoc thong thuong ma con tich hop day du quy trinh hoc tap, on luyen va danh gia. Viec ket hop thanh toan, tien do hoc tap, bai test, chung chi va AI giup website phu hop voi cac nen tang dao tao ngon ngu hien dai, dac biet cho cac khoa hoc luyen thi va ren luyen ky nang viet.
